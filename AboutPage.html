About the Author



Rita Streatfield is a proud grandmother of six grandchildren and one step-grandchild. She began writing about the adventures of Shiko during the first COVID-19 lockdown, and her love of the characters is keeping this series going.


After working with children of all ages throughout her life, she noticed a lack of representation of non-white characters in children's factors.
